1: Introduction & Project Context

This case study explores how Citibike, New York City's largest bike-sharing program, leveraged data analytics in 2022 to uncover actionable insights for improving urban mobility, user experience, and operational efficiency.

Project Goal: Analyze ridership patterns and provide recommendations to optimize service and support sustainable transportation.

Role: Data Analyst (project lead, responsible for data analysis, visualization, and reporting)

2: Problem Definition

How can Citibike optimize its service to increase ridership, improve user satisfaction, and support sustainable transportation in NYC?

What are the key factors influencing ridership patterns?

3: Data Collection & Cleaning

Data Sources

  • Primary Data: Citibike public trip records for 2022
  • Weather Data: NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information
  • Tools: Python (pandas, numpy), SQLite, Tableau Prep

Data Cleaning Process

  • Removed duplicate ride IDs and null station codes
  • Filtered out trips with durations <60 seconds or >24 hours
  • Aligned weather data with trip timestamps
  • Converted temperature units (°F to °C) for consistency

5: Modeling, Results & Lessons Learned

Key Insights

  • Seasonal Patterns: Ridership peaks align with spring and summer seasons
  • Trip Characteristics: Most trips are short (<30 minutes), emphasizing the role of Citibike in daily commuting
  • Station Distribution: High-usage stations are concentrated in commercial and transit areas
  • Weather Dependency: Weather, especially temperature, significantly influences daily ridership levels

Recommendations

  1. Capacity Optimization: Expand bike and dock availability at high-traffic stations during peak hours
  2. Off-Peak Promotion: Promote off-peak and weekend usage through targeted campaigns and incentives
  3. Weather Resilience: Enhance weather-resilient infrastructure, such as covered stations
  4. Dynamic Rebalancing: Implement dynamic rebalancing strategies based on demand forecasts
  5. Targeted Marketing: Continue monitoring user demographics to tailor services effectively

Lessons Learned

The analysis confirms that ridership is heavily influenced by seasonal, temporal, and weather factors. Understanding these patterns enables Citibike to make data-driven decisions for service optimization and strategic planning.
