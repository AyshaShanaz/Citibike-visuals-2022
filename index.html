NYC Citibike 2022 Analysis

Data-Driven Insights for Urban Mobility

Executive Summary

This analysis examines NYC Citibike usage patterns throughout 2022, revealing key insights about urban mobility trends, seasonal variations, and user behavior patterns.

Key Findings

  • Peak usage occurs during morning and evening commute hours
  • Summer months show highest ridership with 25% increase over winter
  • Manhattan stations have the highest turnover rates
  • Average trip duration is 12.5 minutes
